## Quick Reference

**Player 1 (melee):** Shadowheart + Minthara — you control both melee characters. Stay grouped for Minthara's Aura of Protection (+4 saves to nearby allies).

**Player 2 (ranged):** Visara + Astarion — your friend controls both ranged characters. Visara locks enemies down with Extended Command, Astarion deletes priority targets from stealth.

### Key Evil Choices

| When | Choice | Reward |
|:-----|:-------|:-------|
| Act 1 | Raid the Emerald Grove | Minthara joins as permanent companion in Act 2 |
| Act 2 | Kill Strange Ox at Last Light Inn | Hat of Fire Acuity (Visara's core item) |
| Act 2 | Shadowheart kills the Nightsong | Shar's Spear of Evening |
| Act 3 | Murder Tribunal — accept Bhaal | Bhaalist Armour (doubles piercing damage) |
| Act 3 | House of Grief — SH kills parents | Shar's Chosen |
