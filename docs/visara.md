# Visara Leveling Guide - Fire Sorlock

**Final Build:** 11 Red Draconic Sorcerer / 1 Fiend Warlock
**Role:** Ranged caster (controlled by friend) | Gear Priority #1
**Starting Stats:** 17 CHA, 16 DEX, 14 CON (dump STR, INT)
**No respecs needed.**

> **Hag's Hair goes to Shadowheart, NOT Visara.**
> Start 17 CHA. Take Elemental Adept: Fire as 2nd feat (no archer support for Arsonist's Oil).
> Mirror of Loss +2 CHA in Act 3 brings you to 19 CHA. With Acuity at 10 stacks your DC is 30+ which is unresistable.

---

## Level-by-Level Progression

### Level 1: Sorcerer 1 (Red Draconic)
- **Cantrips:** Friends, Minor Illusion, Fire Bolt, Ray of Frost
- **Spells:** Shield, Sleep
- **Notes:** CON save proficiency from starting Sorcerer. Sleep clears early fights.

### Level 2: Sorcerer 2
- **Metamagic:** Twinned, Extended
- **Spell:** Magic Missile
- **Notes:** Twinned for Haste later. Extended for 2-turn Command later.

### Level 3: Sorcerer 3
- **Metamagic:** Quickened
- **Spell:** Scorching Ray. Replace Sleep with Enhance Ability.
- **Notes:** Scorching Ray is your core damage spell forever. Each ray procs riders separately.

### Level 4: Sorcerer 4
- **Feat:** Dual Wielder
- **Spell:** Hold Person
- **Notes:** Enables dual-wielding staves (Spellsparkler + Melf's First Staff in Act 1).

### Level 5: Sorcerer 5
- **Spell:** Haste
- **Notes:** Twinned Haste on self + another damage dealer.

### Level 6: Sorcerer 6 -- Elemental Affinity
- **Spell:** Fireball. Replace Magic Missile with Counterspell.
- **Notes:** Major power spike. +CHA mod to fire damage from Elemental Affinity.

### Level 7: Warlock 1 (Fiend) -- BUILD ONLINE
- **Spell:** Command
- **Features:** Dark One's Blessing (temp HP on kill)
- **Notes:** All 3 core spells acquired. Extended Command locks enemies 2 turns.

### Level 8: Sorcerer 7
- **Spell:** Daylight
- **Notes:** Daylight activates Callous Glow Ring (+1d4 radiant per ray).

### Level 9: Sorcerer 8
- **Feat:** Elemental Adept: Fire
- **Spell:** Dimension Door
- **Notes:** Fire damage can't roll 1s, ignores fire resistance. Best pick without archer support.

### Level 10: Sorcerer 9
- **Spell:** Hold Monster
- **Notes:** Universal control. Held targets auto-crit in melee, advantage on spell attacks.

### Level 11: Sorcerer 10
- **Spell:** Telekinesis
- **Metamagic:** Careful
- **Notes:** Careful Fireball protects melee allies.

### Level 12: Sorcerer 11
- **Spell:** Chain Lightning
- **Notes:** Level 6 spell slot. Chain Lightning for fire-immune enemies. Flight for positioning.

## Standard Gameplay Loop

1. **If not getting Haste from support:** Twinned Haste on self + another damage dealer.
2. **Ensure Daylight is up** (activates Callous Glow Ring bonus damage per ray).
3. **Disable Spellmight Gloves.**
4. **Quickened Scorching Ray (level 4+)** at a high-HP target to stack Acuity to 10. Don't kill it.
5. **Enable Spellmight Gloves.**
6. **Choose:** damage (more Scorching Ray / Fireball) or control (Extended Command on everything).

---

## Key Mechanics

**Heat (Markoheshkir):** Flame of Wrath generates Heat stacks. Click Heat Convergence after each cast to add Heat damage to next spell. Don't stand in oil.

**Scorching Ray:** Each ray is a separate damage source. Each procs ALL riders individually (Elemental Affinity, Callous Glow, Spineshudder, etc.). Each ray generates 2 Acuity stacks.

**Extended Command:** Lasts 2 turns instead of 1. Can lock down 20+ enemies indefinitely by cycling Command: Grovel / Halt on different groups.

**Spellmight Gloves:** -5 spell attack for +1d8 damage per spell. Toggle OFF for initial Acuity-building Scorching Ray, toggle ON after Acuity is stacked (the +10 from Acuity more than covers the -5).

**Metamagic Priority:** Quickened (damage as bonus action) > Extended (2-turn Command) > Careful (protect melee allies from Fireball) > Twinned (Haste).
